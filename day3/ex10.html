Reordering Columns with Order Classes

1. Default Order (no reordering)

First (col-md-6)

Appears first in HTML and on screen

Second (col-md-6)

Appears second in HTML and on screen

2. Reordered on Medium Screens (order-md-*)

First column is reordered to appear second on medium screens and up

First in HTML (order-md-2)

Appears SECOND on md and larger screens

Second in HTML (order-md-1)

Appears FIRST on md and larger screens

3. Three-Column Reordering

Column 1 (order-md-3)

Appears THIRD on medium screens

Column 2 (order-md-1)

Appears FIRST on medium screens

Column 3 (order-md-2)

Appears SECOND on medium screens

4. Mobile vs Desktop Order

Column appears first on mobile, last on desktop screens

Main Content (order-md-2)

Appears first on mobile, second on desktop

Sidebar (order-md-1)

Appears second on mobile, first on desktop
