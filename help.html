Getting Started

Welcome to the Community Event Portal! Here are some basic steps to get you started:

  • Browse Events: Navigate to the "Events" section to see upcoming community events.
  • View Gallery: Check the "Gallery" section to see photos from past events.
  • Register: Use the "Register" section to sign up for an event you'd like to attend.
  • Provide Feedback: Share your experience in the "Feedback" section after attending an event.

To register for an event:

  • Click the "Register" link in the navigation menu.
  • Fill in all required fields marked with an asterisk (*).
  • Select your preferred event type from the dropdown menu.
  • The event fee will be displayed automatically.
  • Click "Submit Registration" to confirm your registration.

Note: Your email address will be used to send you event details and updates.

Viewing Event Photos

Our event gallery showcases photos from past community events:

  • Navigate to the "Gallery" section.
  • Hover over any image to see a preview.
  • Double-click any image to view it in a larger size.
  • Click the dark background to close the enlarged view.
  • Full Name: Your complete name as you'd like it to appear on event materials.
  • Email: A valid email address for event notifications.
  • Event Date: Your preferred date to attend the event.
  • Event Type: The category of event you're interested in.
  • Phone Number: Optional contact number (must be in valid format).
  • Message: Any special requirements or questions about the event.

You can save your preferred event type for future visits:

  • Go to the "Preferences" section.
  • Select your preferred event type from the dropdown.
  • Your preference will be automatically saved to your browser.
  • On your next visit, your preference will be automatically selected.
  • Use "Clear All Preferences" to reset your saved preferences.

Finding Nearby Events

  • Navigate to the "Nearby" section.
  • Click "Find Nearby Events" button.
  • Your browser will ask for permission to access your location.
  • Grant permission to see your coordinates and nearby events.
  • Privacy: Your location data is only used to find nearby events and is not stored.

Providing Event Feedback

  • Go to the "Feedback" section.
  • Select the event you attended.
  • Write your detailed feedback about your experience.
  • Click "Submit Feedback" to send your comments.
  • Your feedback helps us organize better events in the future.

Watching Event Videos

  • Navigate to the "Video" section.
  • Use the video player controls to play, pause, and adjust volume.
  • Watch in fullscreen by clicking the fullscreen button.
  • A status message will appear when the video is ready to play.

Browser Compatibility

This portal works best with modern web browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

    • Important: For location features to work, make sure your browser has permission to access your location.

    Troubleshooting

    Images not loading?

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Refresh the page using Ctrl+R or Cmd+R.
  • Clear your browser cache.

    • Form not submitting?

    • Ensure all required fields (marked with *) are filled.
    • Check that your email address is valid.
    • Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.

    Location not working?

    • Grant permission when the browser asks for your location.
    • Check your browser's location settings.
    • Try using a different browser.
